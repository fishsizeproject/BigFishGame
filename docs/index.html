The sustainable fishing game

Suitable for ages 10+

Best for 2-5 players

The BigFish card game is for everyone interested in fun card games, fishing or marine life.

To win the game you must find the best fishing strategy to collect points from catching fish, while keeping your fishing spot healthy and protected from natural disasters and other players.

Playing the game

Big fish are worth more points than small fish, so players want to catch them. But the presence of big fish also allows players to draw new cards, spawn new fish and also grow them. The player has to find a balance between collecting points by catching fish and preserving big fish for good fishing opportunities in the future.

In addition to fishing, other natural and human-induced events occur throughout the game: fisheries research, poor water quality, citizen science, illegal fishing, ocean acidification, sea urchin arrival and others.

These events create unpredictability and new challenges. Players can partly protect their fishing spots by finding the best game strategy.

Main messages

We developed the game because we love fun card games and we love the ocean. We also love big fish.

1. Big fish are important

Preserving the big fish in the oceans, lakes and rivers is important for fish reproduction and resilience, ecosystem balance, for the fun of fishing, or simply because big fish are beautiful.

2. Big fish are mostly gone

Due to commercial and recreational fishing, big fish have mostly vanished from oceans, rivers and lakes.

3. Big fish can return

Fish grow relatively fast and if we take the right actions, we can restore them within our lifetimes and enjoy their beauty and benefits again.
